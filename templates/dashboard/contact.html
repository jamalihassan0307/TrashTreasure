Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I submit trash for collection?

Simply log in to your account, click "Submit Trash", take a photo, describe the waste, and provide your location. We'll assign a rider within minutes!

How are reward points calculated?

Points are awarded based on the type and quantity of waste collected. Recyclable materials earn more points than general waste.

What types of waste do you accept?

We accept most types of household and office waste including paper, plastic, metal, electronics, and organic waste. Hazardous materials are not accepted.

How long does collection take?

Collection typically happens within 2-4 hours of submission. You can track your rider in real-time through the app.
